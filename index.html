Dragon Rider Romance. Magic Academy. Fated Mates.

Welcome to the Dragon Guard Academy—a hidden military magic school where elite dragon riders are trained to defend the realm. If you love enemies-to-lovers romantasy, fated mates with dragon shifters, and slow burn new adult fantasy romance, this series is for you.

Book 1, Fire & Fury, follows a fiery cadet and her ice-veined commander as they fight fate, loss, and a rising darkness in the Dragonspine Mountains. Book 2, Storm & Steel, takes the story beyond the academy walls, where storm magic brews in a royal court teetering on revolution—and two unlikely allies must forge trust before war ignites.

Fire & Fury (Book 1)

An enemies-to-lovers, fated-mates dragon rider romance set in a ruthless academy fantasy where dragons decide who lives, who loves, and who burns. Perfect for fans of Fourth Wing, Iron Flame, and slow-burn new-adult fantasy romance.

Nineteen-year-old Ember Reese enters the storm-lashed Dragon Guard Academy to prove herself. But when a fire dragon claims her in defiance of every rule, she must survive high-stakes trials, a chilling commander named Kai Thorne, and a prophecy older than dragonsong. As darkness breaches the fortress walls, fire and ice must unite—or everything burns.

Storm & Steel (Book 2)

An enemies-to-lovers, political intrigue dragon rider romance set in a treacherous royal court where storms can be weaponized, alliances shift like lightning, and love defies diplomacy. Perfect for fans of Fourth Wing, Iron Flame, and steamy new-adult fantasy romance.

Scholarship student Luna never expected to clash with Prince Darius—until storm magic and revolution put them on the same side. With political conspiracies, royal masquerades, and elemental warfare rising, Luna must decide if she can trust the prince... or if love is the most dangerous game of all.
