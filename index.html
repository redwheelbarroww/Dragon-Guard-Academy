Enter a world of dragons, destiny, and dangerous love in this gripping romantasy series by R.E. Stormridge.

Book 1: Fire & Fury

Ember Reese is bonded to an ancient fire dragon that defies academy rules—and sparks an enemies-to-lovers inferno with her icy commander. Together, they must face deadly trials, forbidden desire, and a war rising beyond the mountains. Can fire and ice stand together before everything burns?

Book 2: Storm & Steel

Storm-wielding Luna is sent to a royal court teeming with secrets, where her bond with a reluctant prince may be the only hope to stop a revolution. In a world of weaponized weather and treacherous alliances, love is the most dangerous power of all.
