Original Arc

When faith meets fire, and earth calls to sky, some love stories shake the very foundations of the world. Together, their opposing elements form an unprecedented partnership as the Dragon King awakens and darkness spreads. Can a pacifist healer and a grounded sky rider fight for love, faith, and the future of dragon magic?

About the Series

Dragon Guard Academy is a slow-burn romantasy series filled with dragon riders, elemental magic, fated mates, and high-stakes battles between love, loyalty, and legacy. If you loved Fourth Wing and Iron Flame, you'll feel right at home in these pages.

Read the series in order: Fire & Fury, Storm & Steel, Shadow & Light, Ice & Ember, Earth & Sky, Dragon’s Heart, Flame & Frost, Thunder & Stone, Prism & Shadow.
