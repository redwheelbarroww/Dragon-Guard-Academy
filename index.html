Enemies-to-lovers. Fated mates. Dragon fire.
In a ruthless military academy hidden in the Dragonspine Mountains, dragons choose their riders—and their fates. When a volatile fire dragon bonds with cadet Ember Reese, she’s thrust into a forbidden connection with her ice-cold commander, Kai Thorne.
