About the Series

Dragon Guard Academy is a slow-burn romantasy series filled with dragon riders, elemental magic, fated mates, and high-stakes battles between love, loyalty, and legacy. If you loved Fourth Wing and Iron Flame, you'll feel right at home in these pages.

In Book 1, a fire dragon upends everything at a brutal mountain academy. In Book 2, a storm-wielding ambassador must survive a treacherous royal court. Each book is its own love story, with a wider arc connecting the series through the fate of dragons and the fragile realm they protect.
